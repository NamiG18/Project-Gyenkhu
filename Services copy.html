Project Gyenkhu primarily focuses on advocacy of environmental conservation by educating people through videos, documentaries and DIY (DO It Yourself) ideas to reuse plastics to reduce plastic in the environment, thus reducing various problems of pollution and climate change associated with plastics. We also write blogs on current environmental issues in the hope that we will be able to tell the world that "The Problem of Plastic is Real and has Become a HEADLINE", so as to bring realization to every individual to re-define their "plastic using habits". Furthermore we kindle an awareness of liablity amongst all generations to become mindful of their action which are either harmful or beneficial to Mother Earth. Finally Project Gyenkhu highlights on galvanizing and encouraging communities to clean their environment and ensure cleanliness for all times to come.
